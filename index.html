📝 About

I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in the School of Information Science and Technology at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) , advised by Prof. Li Li and Prof. Houqiang Li , and working closely with Zhendong Wang. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree from the School of Gifted Young (SCGY) at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2023. I previously worked as a research intern at Tencent Hunyuan Research and the Alibaba Taobao & TMall Group. During my internship at Tencent Hunyuan Research, I collaborated closely with Shuyang Gu, Zigang Geng, and Mengde Xu. At Alibaba Taobao & TMall Group, I worked closely with Daowen Li, Kai Li, and Ying Chen .

My research focuses on image and video generation, multimodal large language models, and image and video compression.

I am expected to graduate in June 2028 with a Ph.D. degree. I am actively seeking internship opportunities in algorithm research or engineering in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision. If you are interested in my background and research experience, please do not hesitate to contact me.
